Summary

Serenity is designed to be calm, minimal, and respectful of privacy. We collect only what we need to operate the app, and we avoid using your personal reflection as a source of profiling or optimization.

Key promise: Journal entries are private and are not used for AI analysis or personalization.

What we collect

  • Account information (e.g., email, sign-in method) to help you access your space.
  • App settings (e.g., sound preference, text size) to keep your experience consistent.
  • Journal entries (your writing) if you use the Journal.
  • Ritual preferences (e.g., last selected ritual) to support continuity.
  • Basic technical data (e.g., device type, crash logs) to improve reliability.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not run ads based on your reflections.
  • We do not create psychological profiles from your Journal.
  • We do not use Journal entries to train AI models.
  • We do not share your Journal with other users.

How AI is used

AI is used only in Companion (Chat) to respond to what you choose to share in that space. AI is not used in the Journal.

  • Companion (Chat): AI may reflect, ask gentle questions, and respond one step at a time.
  • Journal: No AI analysis, summarization, sentiment scoring, or prompts based on your entries.
  • Rituals: No AI conversation during rituals.

Tip: If you want an experience with no AI involvement at all, use Rituals and Journal only.

How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide access to your account and saved content.
  • Store your Journal entries and preferences so they persist across devices (if enabled).
  • Maintain app stability (bug fixes, crash diagnostics).
  • Protect the service from abuse and unauthorized access.

Data storage & security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including encryption in transit and access controls. No system is perfect, but security is treated as a core requirement.

  • In transit: Encrypted communications between your device and our servers.
  • At rest: Stored data is protected using industry-standard security practices.
  • Access: Restricted to authorized personnel and systems needed to operate the app.

Retention

We keep your content for as long as your account is active, unless you delete it. You can request deletion at any time.

  • Journal entries: Kept until you delete them or request account deletion.
  • Chat history: May be stored to support continuity (optional), but is not used for profiling.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs may be retained for a limited period for reliability and debugging.

You can fine-tune this section once you decide exact retention windows and whether chat history is stored.

Your choices & controls

  • Export: Request a copy of your data (where supported).
  • Delete entries: Delete journal entries within the app.
  • Delete account: Request account deletion and associated data removal.
  • Sound: Enable/disable ritual sound in settings.
